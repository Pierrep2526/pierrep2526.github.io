📖 What is meshdeck?

Meshdeck is open-source firmware for the LilyGo T-Deck. It transforms your hardware into a portable communication tool. Use it for text messaging, viewing offline maps, and managing mesh networks. You do not need to install complex software on your computer to get started.

🚀 Getting Started

Follow these steps to update your device. These instructions work on Windows computers using the Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge web browsers.

1. Prepare your computer

Ensure your computer remains connected to the internet. We use a web tool to send the files directly to your T-Deck.

2. Connect your device

Connect your LilyGo T-Deck to your computer using a high-quality USB-C cable. If the screen on your device does not turn on, check your cable connection.

3. Access the flasher

Visit the official repository below to access the integrated web flasher:

Launch Web Flasher

4. Install the firmware

Once the page loads, click the “Connect” button on the screen. Select your device from the pop-up list. Choose the latest firmware version from the menu and click “Install.” Keep your device connected throughout the process. The status bar will show the progress. Do not unplug the cable until the screen indicates that the installation is complete.

🛠 Features

  • Chat: Send secure encrypted messages to other mesh users.
  • Offline Maps: Navigate without cellular data or Wi-Fi.
  • Mesh Tools: Manage your network nodes and signal strength.
  • Radio Presets: Quickly configure channels for local groups.
  • SOS Beacon: Broadcast your coordinates during emergencies.

💡 Troubleshooting

If your browser does not see your device, try a different USB port. Some USB cables provide power but do not transmit data; verify that your cable supports data transfer. If you use a corporate computer, check if your security settings prevent web-based hardware access.
